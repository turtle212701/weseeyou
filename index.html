The Traitor


We have all read about The Transmogrification, The Quest and The Ascendency. But have you read the chapter in between The Quest and The Ascendency?

The Mutant Shibas met that night in the Skull Crusher Den. All eyes were on Ziploch. Many were silent, a few were growling softly amongst themselves. Ziploch growled loudly and everyone fell silent.

Ziploch

"Wheres the scoundrel?"

4 Shibas dragged a member to the front of the pack and threw him at Ziploch's feet.

Ziploch

"Speak you filth! You were tasked with finding the 7th crystal fragment months ago. WHERE IS IT???"

"I don't answer to mutant rejects."

Grimscrow

Ziploch

"YOU SHOULD KNOW WHO YOU ARE SPEAKING TO!!!"

Ziploch turns to the members who brought Grimscrow.

Ziploch

"Give him a taste. Ten lashes for speaking to me like that. And another five for good measure."

The four Shibas dragged Grimscrow to the corner of the den where there was a whip with contaminated Ranklet venom. (ranklet venom was used for carving symbols into stone, in its contaminated form ranklet venom causes deeper burn grooves than usual and is prone to ignite when brought in contact with stixxer fumes)

Khaotichnyy

"Tie him up."

As the helpless Grimscrow was tied, Khaotichnyy flexed the whip and brought it down on Grimscrow.

"You sick psychopath! I'll have your inards for that!"

Grimscrow

Khaotichnyy

"Oh, you will, will you?."

He brought the whip down fourteen more times until Grimscrows' throat was hoarse from howling. Many Shibas were wincing from the noise. Though a few were all smiles.

Khaotichnyy

"Heal from that you mongrel!"

They dragged Grimscrow back back to the center of the den and threw him at Ziploch's feet.

Ziploch

"May you watch you tongue next time or it'll be fifty lashes and you'll be mute. Now, what have you found out about the seventh crystal fragment?"

"I found an outcast beyond the wetlands, a Gode Collective. He wandered here looking for the other six fragments."

Grimscrow

Howls of rage broke out among members of the pack.

Ziploch

"You traitor! Have you forgotten what the Gode Collective threatened to do? Or is your memory wavering along with your loyalty?"

"I was doing my job! Do you want that fragment or not?! We can trust him. He's ancestors messed up orders and was kicked out by the Gode Collective. He attacked me, but the serum in me was enough to overpower him. After he'd calmed down, he told me that he'd found the a crystal fragment in the Traitor Corpse Temple, in Switzerland and that it was common knowledge that the six remaining fragments were here. He said he'd been looking for the pieces so he could get revenge on the Gode Collective for banishing his ancestors. When he heard we were trying to unite the crystal he proposed a trade."

Grimscrow

Ziploch

"What trade is this?"

"He wants the other crystal fragments to create the zenith serum to mutate himself so he can take revenge on the collective. And after he's done, he'll give us the final crystal fragment."

Grimscrow

There was a lot of murmered growling.

Ziploch

"And what if he uses the serum against us? We don't know how the serum affects the DNA of the Gode Collective members. It might make them stronger than us. We cannot have this risk. We will not do the trade."

"The Balavrak are soon going to converge on our hiding place and seize the crystal shards. Good luck finding them again. I'd bet that whipping loser's life it'd be a couple hundred years until they're found again."

Grimscrow

Ziploch

"We still risk extinction of an entire generation of Mutant Shibas if the Gode Collective get their hands on the serum."

"Very well than, enjoy living your lives in secret."

Grimscrow

Grimscrow ran form the den amidst the growls of other Shibas.

Ziploch

"Tregoskiyy follow him at a distance. The rest of you listen to me."

The Shiba named 'Tregoskiyy' sprinted out at top speed while the rest of the members turned towards Ziploch.

Grimscrow ran five miles west to an abandoned cave, completely oblivious that he was being followed Tregoskiyy. He entered the cave and met the Gode Collective reject, Muchit'

Muchit'

"Were you followed?"

"No."

Grimscrow

Muchit'

"what did they say?"

"They said the exactly what I expected, that you might double cross us."

Grimscrow

Muchit'

"Why would I want to join the people who kicked my ancestors out?"

"I have no idea."

Grimscrow

Muchit'

"How much do the Balavrak suspect?"

"They're closing in on the other fragments, someone gave them a very important clue."

Grimscrow

Muchit'

"You fool! If the Balavrak find the other fragments they'll scatter them again and I'll never get my revenge! Why did you give them a clue?"

"Calm your tits! It wasn't a very helpful clue."

Grimscrow

Muchit'

"I still dont understand why you can't get me the other fragments."

"I've told you before you idiot, they are always guarded by the rat droppings eater, Khaotichnyy and his goonies. Strong as I am, I cannot overpower all of them. Wheres your piece?"

Grimscrow

Muchit'

"Tangant region, Assaba. or if you're a coordinate freak, 18.078376, -11.442785 "

"Why didn't you get it here?"

Grimscrow

Muchit'

"Thought I'd be intercepted-"

"You were right!"

Tregoskiyy

They both whipped around to see Tregoskiyy at the cave entrance snarling.

"How much have you heard?"

Grimscrow

Tregoskiyy

"Enough to know that you are a traitor"

"What are you going to do Tregoskiyy"

Grimscrow

Tregoskiyy

"Silence you and that reject. Permanently"

Tregoskiyy pounced at Muchit' and with a quick slash, he knocked his head off.

"What have you done you imbecile?"

Grimscrow

Tregoskiyy and Grimscrow both ran at each other. What Grimscrow didn't know however, was that Tregoskiyy's claws were laced with Stixxer fluid. They both fought, scratching and biting until a claw scraped the open wound on Grimscrows snout. He immediately began to smoulder, putrid smoke was filling the cave. Tregoskiyy ran out just as Grimscrows fur begun to blaze. Tregoskiyy could still hear the howls of pain in his mind when he reached the den again and was repeating everything he heard to Ziploch.
